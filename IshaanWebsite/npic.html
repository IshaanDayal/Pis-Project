So what's it all about

There has been a rise in awareness amongst the people about mental health issues and how they should be treated and given importance as an individual commit to his physical health because along with a healthy body, a healthy and happy mind is also essential for holistic well-being.

Keeping up with the spirit and consciousness matured amongst people about mental health through the recently concluded Mental Health Day, we base our project for the well-being of people suffering from attention deficit hyperactivity disorder (ADHD).

Project Description

Our aim is to utilize LED lights and create artistic patterns and designs which helps soothe the minds of someone suffering from ADHD. Through this project we aim to bring some reassuring and calmness to their minds and they are constantly having concentration problems which keeps their minds stressed and active at all times. Through this effort and gesture we hope to provide certain amounts of relaxation and relief to the onlooker.

Our project is not limited for people having ADHD but is open for appreciation from every observer. Through this endeavor we combine technology and art with social consciousness with the intention of aid for humanity.
